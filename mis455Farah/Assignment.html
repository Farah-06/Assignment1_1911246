About Winnie the Pooh

Winnie-the-Pooh is a fictional anthropomorphic teddy bear created by
English author A. A. Milne and English illustrator E. H. Shepard.

Winnie-the-Pooh first appeared by name in a children's story commissioned by
London's Evening News for Christmas Eve 1925.



About the Characters


Piglet

Piglet is a fictional character from A. A. Milne's Winnie-the-Pooh books. Piglet is Winnie‑the‑Pooh's closest friend amongst all the toys and animals featured in the stories.


Tiger

Tigger is a fictional character, an anthropomorphic stuffed tiger. He was originally introduced in the 1928 story collection The House at Pooh Corner.


Rabbit

Rabbit knows how to have fun, often spending time drinking tea or ice skating, but when he gets riled up.
